|-----------|---------|

| **Internet** | 4G mobile data, occasional WiFi |

### Background

Chibuzo handles all marketing for Prime Properties, a growing agency in Lekki. He manages Instagram, creates listing posts in Canva, and coordinates with freelance designers for landing pages. Most leads come through WhatsApp, and he spends hours responding to "Is this still available?" messages.

### Goals
- Create landing pages quickly for new property listings
- Generate more qualified WhatsApp leads (not just "price please?")
- Make the agency look as professional as the big players like Landwey
- Reduce time spent on back-and-forth messaging
- Enable agents to share professional pages instead of Instagram posts

### Pain Points
- Current designer charges â‚¦80,000â€“â‚¦150,000 per landing page
- Takes 5-7 days to get a single page done
- Pages don't work well on mobile (90% of their audience)
- No easy way to add WhatsApp buttons
- Templates on Wix don't understand Nigerian property types
- Has to manually convert sqft to sqm for every listing

### Quote
> "I post a new listing on Instagram and get 50 DMs saying 'price?' and 'location?'. If I had a proper landing page, people would get the info first and only serious buyers would message me."

### Tech Stack
- **Design**: Canva Pro
- **Communication**: WhatsApp Business, Instagram DM
- **Email**: Gmail (rarely used for leads)
- **Ads**: Instagram/Facebook ads
- **CRM**: Excel spreadsheet
- **Website**: WordPress (rarely updated)

### A Day in Chibuzo's Life

1. **8:00 AM** - Checks WhatsApp for overnight inquiries
2. **9:00 AM** - Posts new listing on Instagram
3. **10:00 AM** - Gets 20 DMs asking for price/location
4. **11:00 AM** - Sends same info to each person individually
5. **2:00 PM** - Tries to create landing page in Canva (gives up)
6. **4:00 PM** - Sends brief to freelance designer
7. **5:00 PM** - More WhatsApp inquiries
8. **9:00 PM** - Still responding to messages

### What Would Delight Chibuzo
- Generate a page in 5 minutes that he can share via WhatsApp
- Page automatically shows price, location, and photos
- WhatsApp button pre-filled with property name
- Mobile-perfect design
- Naira pricing that looks professional (â‚¦85,000,000)

## 9. Nigerian Market Insights

### Communication Preferences

| Channel | Usage | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **WhatsApp** | 95%+ | Primary for all business |
| **Instagram DM** | 70% | Discovery and initial contact |
| **Phone calls** | 60% | Serious buyers call |
| **Email** | 20% | Enterprise/corporate only |
| **SMS** | 10% | Rarely used for leads |

### Payment Preferences

| Method | Usage | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| **Card (Paystack)** | 50% | Growing, especially younger users |
| **Bank Transfer** | 40% | Still very common |
| **USSD** | 10% | For non-smartphone users |

### Device Usage

| Device | Percentage |
|--------|------------|
| **Mobile (Android)** | 65% |
| **Mobile (iOS)** | 20% |
| **Desktop** | 15% |
